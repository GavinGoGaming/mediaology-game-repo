Parsec on Glacier

First of all: What's Parsec? Parsec is a remote desktop tool that allows you to access a home machine remotely.
Setting Up Parsec On your Home Machine 1. Head to parsec.app 2. Register and go to dash.parsec.app 3. Click Downloads to install Parsec 4. Login on the app and setup. On your Viewing Machine (non-glacier instructions, scroll for glacier) 1. Head to web.parsec.app and log in 2. Find your computer and "Connect"
Integration with Glacier Glacier 0.9.0 and below will not load the page. 1. Open Microsoft Edge 2. Head to https://web.parsec.app/ 3. Login to your account 4. Find your computer, press "Connect"
